Timberframe Terrace - Tacoma, WA

This covered deck in Tacoma, WA was built with premium Deckorators Khaya decking, delivering a rich, tropical wood look with the durability and low maintenance of high-performance decking. The project was fully permitted and engineered, ensuring it meets all local building codes for long-term safety and quality.

The deck features a striking timber-frame design with 4x10 rafters, 6x6 posts, and 6x8 glulam beams. Underneath, 2x6 car decking provides a polished, finished appearance, while the black aluminum cable railing adds a modern, sleek touch. Designed for both style and function, this covered deck creates a comfortable outdoor living space for year-round enjoyment.

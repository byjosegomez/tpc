Sky Island Patio Cover In Bonney Lake

This permitted lean-to patio cover in Bonney Lake was built to create a comfortable, functional extension of the home’s outdoor living space. Designed with a clean roofline that ties directly into the existing structure, the cover provides reliable protection from the elements while maintaining a seamless, natural look.

The ceiling is finished with stained pine tongue-and-groove, adding warmth and texture overhead. Recessed lighting and a ceiling fan were installed to improve airflow and make the space usable day and night. The support posts were wrapped in PVC for a low-maintenance, durable finish that will hold up beautifully over time.

Fully permitted and built to meet city requirements, this Bonney Lake project delivers both style and long-term structural integrity.

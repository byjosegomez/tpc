About Tacoma Patio Covers

At Tacoma Patio Covers, we design and build custom decks and patio covers that turn ordinary backyards into beautiful, functional living spaces. With years of hands-on experience, we focus on craftsmanship that not only enhances your home’s exterior but stands strong for years to come.

A Hands-On Approach
As an owner-operated company, I’m personally involved in every project from the first consultation to the final walkthrough. This direct involvement ensures attention to detail, clear communication, and a finished product that reflects both quality workmanship and your unique vision.

Knowledge & Expertise
Building a deck or patio cover requires more than just construction—it requires understanding materials, weather performance, and structural integrity. We work with high-quality hardwoods, composite decking, aluminum, and polycarbonate systems to create outdoor spaces that combine durability, functionality, and refined design.

Our Commitment to Quality & Warranty
Every project is completed with pride and precision, treated as detailed finish exterior work from start to finish. We stand behind our craftsmanship with a lifetime workmanship warranty for as long as you own your home, giving you confidence that your investment is built to last.

Let’s Build Something Beautiful
Whether you’re looking for a relaxing backyard retreat or a sturdy patio cover for year-round comfort, we’re ready to bring your ideas to life. Let’s create an outdoor space that adds value, beauty, and lasting enjoyment to your home.

Jose Gomez Founder of Tacoma Patio Covers

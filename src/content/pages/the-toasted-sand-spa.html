The Toasted Sand Spa - Olympia

The deck features a custom pergola surrounding the hot tub, creating a cozy and stylish backyard retreat. Trex Toasted Sand composite decking provides a durable, low-maintenance surface that complements the pergola and overall design.

Black aluminum picket railings were added where needed for safety and visual definition, blending seamlessly with the decking to create a functional, inviting space perfect for relaxing or entertaining around the hot tub.

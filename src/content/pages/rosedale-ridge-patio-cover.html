Rosedale Ridge Patio Cover In Gig harbor

This custom patio cover was designed to seamlessly blend with the home while adding both style and functionality to the outdoor space. The ceiling features rich, stained pine tongue-and-groove boards that bring warmth and natural character, complemented by integrated recessed lighting for a clean, modern finish and added evening usability.

The structural beams and support posts were wrapped and painted white, creating a crisp contrast against the stained wood ceiling and tying in beautifully with the home’s existing trim. To ensure a cohesive look, we installed architectural shingles that perfectly match the house roofline, making the addition feel original to the structure.

This project was fully permitted and built to code, giving the homeowner peace of mind that the patio cover is structurally sound, professionally installed, and compliant with local building regulations.

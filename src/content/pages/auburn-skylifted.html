Auburn Skylifted - Auburn, WA

This impressive second-story covered deck in Auburn, WA was built using Skylift roof risers, allowing us to achieve a tall, elevated structure while maintaining stability and safety. The project features premium materials, including sleek black hardware, creating a modern and durable outdoor living space.

The deck cover was challenging to construct due to its height and complexity, but our team successfully engineered and assembled the structure with precision. This project demonstrates our ability to handle all kinds of covered deck designs, from simple patio covers to intricate multi-level structures, delivering functional and visually striking outdoor spaces.

The Modern Pergola in East Tacoma

This expansive patio cover was designed to create a striking and functional outdoor living area. Spanning 40 feet with only two 8x8 cedar posts, the structure achieves an open, airy layout while maintaining exceptional strength, thanks to an 8x12 I-beam engineered to carry the load.

The posts and beams were painted black for a bold, modern contrast, while the rest of the structure was finished with Sherwin Williams solid stain in Lodge Brown, adding warmth and durability. Skylift roof risers securely attach the cover to the house, and a Cover Your Pergola roof system provides reliable, low-maintenance protection while allowing natural light to filter through.

This project blends impressive structural engineering with thoughtful design, delivering a large, functional, and visually appealing outdoor space.

Artondale Glow In Gig Harbor

The Artondale Glow deck was crafted using TimberTech Reserve Reclaimed Chestnut, combining the rich, warm tones of the decking with sleek black aluminum rails and cable railing for a modern, sophisticated look. One side features hideaway screens to provide privacy while maintaining an open, airy feel.

Custom lighting along the posts and stairs creates a beautiful glow at night, highlighting the deck’s design and making it a perfect outdoor space for evening entertaining, relaxing, or enjoying the surrounding views.

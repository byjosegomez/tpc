Project Sandstone - Puyallup, WA

Project Sandstone was built using Trex composite decking in Toasted Sand, paired with Coastal Bluff picture-frame boards and a crisp white composite picket railing for a timeless, classic look. Two sets of stairs were added to provide easy access from multiple sides, enhancing functionality and flow for outdoor entertaining.

The deck also features matching Toasted Sand skirting, giving the base a finished, cohesive appearance while maintaining low maintenance and long-lasting durability. This design creates an inviting, polished outdoor space perfect for gatherings or relaxing.

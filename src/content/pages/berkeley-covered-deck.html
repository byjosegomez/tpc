Berkeley Covered Deck - Spanaway, WA

This covered deck in Spanaway, WA was built using Deckorators composite decking, creating a durable and low-maintenance outdoor living space designed for year-round enjoyment. We poured a concrete slab to support the spa and fully hooked it up, seamlessly blending comfort and function into the layout.

To elevate the design, we installed a custom inlay in the center of the deck, which was mirrored above on the pine ceiling for a cohesive, eye-catching detail. Electrical was added for a Traeger grill, and a TV was professionally mounted on the wall, turning this covered deck into a complete outdoor entertainment space perfect for relaxing or hosting.

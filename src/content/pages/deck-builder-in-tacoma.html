Low Maintenance & High Performance

Why Low-Maintenance Decking Is So Popular

Choosing composite and PVC decking makes sense for a number of reasons, including low maintenance and long-term durability. Made from advanced synthetic materials like recycled wood fibers and plastic, or fully synthetic PVC, these decks are strong, stable, and resistant to moisture — making them a smart long-term investment. Composite and PVC decking also come in a variety of styles and colors. Not only do they look great and increase the value of your home, but they also save you time and money by resisting fading, staining, and rot while staying beautiful as they age.

Low-maintenance decking is:

  • Weather Resistant: Built to withstand rain, moisture, and harsh outdoor conditions.
  • No Staining Required: Never needs to be sanded, sealed, or stained like traditional wood.
  • Won’t Splinter or Rot: Designed to resist cracking, splintering, and wood decay.
  • 25–50 Year Warranty: Backed by long-term manufacturer warranties for peace of mind.
  • Easy to Clean: Simple soap-and-water maintenance keeps it looking new.
  • Variety of Colors: Available in multiple modern tones and natural wood looks.
  • Stain Resistant: Engineered to resist spills, fading, and everyday wear.
